Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our training programs

Do I need prior programming experience?
For our Python Fundamentals track, no prior experience is needed. For advanced tracks like Robot Framework or k6, basic programming knowledge is recommended but not required. We provide resources to help you catch up.
What is the time commitment required?
Live sessions are typically 2-3 hours per week in the evening. We recommend dedicating an additional 3-5 hours for practice and projects. All sessions are recorded, so you can learn at your own pace.
Will I receive a certificate upon completion?
Yes! Upon successfully completing the training and assessments, you'll receive a certificate of completion that you can add to your LinkedIn profile and resume.
Can I access course materials after completion?
Absolutely! You get lifetime access to all course materials, including video recordings, code examples, and project templates. You'll also receive updates when we refresh the content.
What tools and software do I need?
All required tools are free and open-source. You'll need a computer (Windows, Mac, or Linux) and we'll guide you through installing Python, IDE, and other necessary tools in the first session.
Is there job placement assistance?
While we don't guarantee job placement, we provide career guidance, resume reviews, and interview preparation. Many of our graduates have successfully transitioned to automation roles.
